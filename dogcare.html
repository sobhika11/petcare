Balanced Diet

Feed your dog high-quality food tailored to their age, size, breed, and activity level. Puppies, adults, and seniors all have different dietary needs.

Avoid feeding your dog harmful human foods like chocolate, grapes, onions, and anything with caffeine or alcohol.

Keep fresh water available at all times, especially after exercise.
